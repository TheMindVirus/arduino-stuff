[Componentry]
The Practice of Neatly and Precisely Aligning and Arranging
Individual Component Parts of a Development Platform
given a certain shaped area or bounding box
to make it Ready for Self-Assembly

[Socketry]
A subspecialty of componentry and gadgetry classes
with specific focus on modular interconnects
between individial parts of a machine
to make it easier to service

[Gadgetry]
Rating + decision-making + selection
of pre-assembled equipment for usage
in a particular context and scenario
looking to optimise certain criteria
