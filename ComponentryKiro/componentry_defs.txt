[Componentry]
The Practice of Neat Alignment and Arrangement
of Individual Parts of a Machine
or Development Platform
Ready for Self-Assembly

[Socketry]
A subspecialty of componentry
with specific focus on modular interconnects
between individial parts of a machine
to make it easier to service

[Gadgetry]
Rating + decision-making + selection
of pre-assembled equipment for usage
in a particular context and scenario
looking to optimise specific critera
